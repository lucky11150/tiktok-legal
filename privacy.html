Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 16, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our service to connect your TikTok account.

Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum data necessary to provide our service:

  • Public video metadata (e.g., title, view count, likes) via TikTok's video.list API scope
  • TikTok user ID and basic profile info (with your consent)

How We Use Your Data

All data is used exclusively to display analytics to you on our platform. We do not:

  • Sell or rent your data
  • Share data with advertisers or third parties
  • Post content on your behalf

Data Security

Access tokens are encrypted and stored securely. You may disconnect your TikTok account at any time to revoke access.
